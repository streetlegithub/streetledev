Leveler!

A simple, free leveling discord bot.

How To Level Up:

How do we get XP?

To increase your XP you can chat (1XP per message).

How many levels are there?

In total you can get to Level 50 if you grind and post enough messages on a server. Scroll down to find these requirements.

How do I check my XP?

To check your current Level & XP you can type /xp or /level.

How do I check another users XP & Level?

You currently cannot check another users XP or Level currently however it is in development. You can, however, check leaderboards using /leaderboard. This will only show the top 10.
